Key Features

Large-scale pre-training

GPT-3.5 was trained on over 57 billion words, which is nearly 4 times the amount used to train GPT-3. This scale of pre-training allows GPT-3.5 to perform well on multiple natural language processing tasks

Improved multi-model learning

GPT-3.5 can handle multiple modalities of information such as text, images, sound, and more. This multi-modal learning allows GPT-3.5 to better understand and process various types of information in the real world.

Higher control and interpretability

GPT-3.5 has higher control and interpretability in its pred iction and generation process. It can achieve specific outpu ts by controlling the inputs and better understand the logic behin d its generated results by analyzing the intermediate states. This fea ture makes GPT-3.5 more applicable in applications that require interpreta bility and control.
